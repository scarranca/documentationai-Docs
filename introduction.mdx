## Overview

Gigstack automates fiscal invoicing and collections for businesses in Mexico. You generate CFDI-compliant invoices automatically whenever you receive payments through integrated payment processors. This eliminates manual billing, ensures SAT compliance, and saves hours of administrative work.

Connect Gigstack to Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, WooCommerce, and more. Monitor your dashboard for real-time metrics on invoices, income, and fiscal reconciliation.

Gigstack suits Mexican businesses handling high-volume payments, such as e-commerce stores, SaaS companies, and service providers. If you spend hours on manual facturación or struggle with SAT compliance, Gigstack automates it all.
