---
title: Can't invite team members
description: Fix problems inviting users to a Gigstack project.
---

## Symptoms

- Invites are not sent or received
- Users don't appear after invite

## Causes

- Wrong email address
- Invitation email in spam
- User is already a member

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email spelling">
    Confirm you entered the correct email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend invite">
    Use the **Resend** button next to the pending invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check spam folder">
    Ask the user to look for the invite in spam/junk.
  </Step>
</Steps>
