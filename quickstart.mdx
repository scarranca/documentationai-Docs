<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- An active Stripe, PayPal, or Shopify account
- A Mexican RFC (Registro Federal de Contribuyentes) for SAT compliance
- Admin access to your payment processor dashboard
</Callout>

## Create Your Gigstack Account

Sign up for Gigstack to automate your CFDI invoicing. The process takes less than 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [https://app.gigstack.pro/register](https://app.gigstack.pro/register) and create your account with your email and password.

    Provide your business details including RFC and company name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Gigstack and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to [https://app.gigstack.pro/login](https://app.gigstack.pro/login) and sign in to access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect a Payment Processor

Gigstack integrates seamlessly with popular processors. Choose your provider below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Stripe" icon="dollar-sign">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Get Webhook Secret" icon="key">
        In your Stripe dashboard, go to Developers > Webhooks. Add endpoint `https://api.gigstack.io/webhooks/stripe`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy Signing Secret">
        Copy the signing secret (starts with `whsec_`).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add to Gigstack">
        In Gigstack Integrations, select Stripe, paste the secret, and save.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <CodeGroup tabs="Webhook Config">
      ````json
      {
        "url": "https://api.gigstack.io/webhooks/stripe",
        "events": ["checkout.session.completed", "payment_intent.succeeded"]
      }
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PayPal" icon="paypal">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enable IPN" icon="settings">
        In PayPal, go to Account Settings > Notifications > Instant Payment Notifications.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set Listener URL">
        Enter `https://api.gigstack.io/webhooks/paypal` as the Notification URL.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect in Gigstack">
        Paste your PayPal email in Gigstack Integrations > PayPal.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Test the connection by making a sandbox payment in your processor. Gigstack will auto-generate a CFDI invoice.
</Callout>

## Issue Your First Automated Invoice

With integration active:

1. Process a test payment in Stripe or PayPal.
2. Gigstack detects it instantly and emits a CFDI.
3. Download the PDF and XML from the Invoices tab.

Monitor status in real-time on the dashboard.

## Basic SAT Compliance Setup

Configure your SAT credentials for legal compliance.

<Expandable title="Advanced SAT Configuration" default-open="false">
  Enter your:
  - Production certificate (.cer, .key, password)
  - e.firma password

  <ParamField header="X-RFC" param-type="string" required="true">
    Your business RFC.
  </ParamField>

  <ParamField body="regimen" param-type="string" required="false">
    Fiscal regimen code (e.g., "616" for general activities).
  </ParamField>

  Test issuance with a sandbox CFDI first.
</Expandable>
